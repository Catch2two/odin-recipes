Lasagna!

An absolutely delicious dish! Crafted with love and Ketchup and Ice cubes.

Ingredients

    Ketchup
    Salt
    Sea Salt
    Candy Corn Saute

      Steps

    • Thaw out the Ketchup
    • Use your foot to flatten out the Ramen Noodle bricks
    • Microwave Thawed out Ketchup for 45 Minutes
    • Mix everything in a Corn Wrap and serve on Ice
