Succulent Meat Pie

Mold Wine, Juiced from Mold Berries.

Ingredients

    salt
    sugar
    Someone elses Wine
    Mayonnaise
    Root Beer

Steps

  • Mix Salt and Sugar in someone elses Wine
  • 2 heaps of mayo tossed in for flavor
  • Filter Root Beer through cheese cloth into Wine
